# Data Source Attribution

Argent's seed data is compiled from the following public sources.
All data is processed offline at build time â€” no runtime API calls are made.

## Wikipedia Film Lists

- **Source:** [List of photographic films](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_photographic_films), [List of discontinued photographic films](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_discontinued_photographic_films)
- **License:** [CC-BY-SA 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/4.0/)
- **Attribution:** Data contributed by Wikipedia contributors.
- **Data used:** Film stock names, brands, ISO speeds, formats, processes, countries of origin.

## Lensfun Database

- **Source:** [lensfun/lensfun](https://github.com/lensfun/lensfun) (data/db directory)
- **License:** Database: [CC-BY-SA 3.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/3.0/); Library: LGPL-3.0 (not used)
- **Attribution:** Data contributed by the Lensfun project and its contributors.
- **Data used:** Camera bodies, lens specifications, mount definitions, and mount compatibility data.

## Cornell University Camera Mount List

- **Source:** [Mounts by Register Distance](https://www.graphics.cornell.edu/~westin/misc/mounts-by-register.html) by Stephen Westin
- **License:** Academic publication of factual data (physical measurements).
- **Attribution:** Stephen Westin, Cornell University.
- **Data used:** Mount register/flange distances and mount diameters.

## Photon Detector Film Data Index

- **Source:** [Film Data](https://photondetector.com/tools_ref/filmdata/)
- **License:** Factual data (edge codes are physical markings on film).
- **Attribution:** Photon Detector (courtesy attribution).
- **Data used:** Film edge codes (DX markings) and large-format notch codes.

## The Big Film Database

- **Source:** [dxdatabase/Open-source-film-database](https://github.com/dxdatabase/Open-source-film-database)
- **License:** [CC-BY-SA 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/4.0/)
- **Attribution:** Data contributed by the DX Database project and its contributors.
- **Data used:** DX codes, film availability status, country of manufacture, and rebrand/relabeling relationships.
